Use these folders for uploaded assets:

- `public/uploads/portraits`: personal portraits and cutout photos for social cards
- `public/uploads/posts`: cover images and visuals for news, blog, and Chairman's Desk posts
- `public/uploads/projects`: project covers and supporting project visuals
- `public/uploads/media`: general gallery assets, downloadable media, and other shared uploads

Use paths in the app like:

- `/uploads/portraits/elly-portrait.jpg`
- `/uploads/posts/news-update-cover.jpg`
- `/uploads/projects/smartwaste-cover.jpg`
- `/uploads/media/event-gallery-01.jpg`
